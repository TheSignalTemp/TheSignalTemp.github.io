The Signal

1 Year Anniversary

When the Sign.al started, Bill, Will, and I never intended to create an entire club. It was simply a series of interviews with alumni who inspired us to be more than what we felt constrained to be at Penn. Only after we realized EVERY student deserved to hear their words of wisdom did we decide to build something to cultivate it. Thus, the Sign.al was created out of love and curiosity, by people who remember we’re a family first, and a club second. Hearing from students every passing year about how Sign.al projects have impacted them for the better makes my heart full. I couldn’t be more proud of the current team keeping the legacy strong.
